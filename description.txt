Wildlife Mod adds new passive animals and plants to add that extra touch of atmosphere to your terraria world. Note that is this an early WIP, and will have a lot more content in the future. Animals include:

Deer
Monkey
Turtle (Has a gold variant)
Dragonfly
Dolphin
Fox
Fairy
Snake (Has a gold variant)

Plants include:

Jaderose
Medustalk
Flux Coral
Bamboo
Gelon
Cattails
Strawberries
Frost Shine
