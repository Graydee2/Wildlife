Wildlife Mod adds new passive animals and plants to add that extra touch of atmosphere to your terraria world. You can make several new potions and get other goodies from the content. Animals include:

Deer
Monkey
Turtle (Has a gold variant)
Dragonfly
Dolphin
Fox
Fairy
Snake (Has a gold variant)
Turkey
Infernal Mosquito
Netch
Flying Coin

Plants include:

Jaderose
Medustalk
Flux Coral
Bamboo
Gelon
Cattails
Strawberries
Frost Shine
Magma Corn
Nymph Berries
